Fufu Recipie

Description

A staple of West African food that you serve with soups and stews

Ingredients

  • fufu mix (because were lazy)
  • water
  • bowl
  • Microwave (also because were lazy)

Steps

  1. add 2-3 cups of water in a bowl
  2. add fufu mix
  3. mix until consistent with no lumps
  4. heat up until it is no more runny
